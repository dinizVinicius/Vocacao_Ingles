Propaedeutic

The propaedeutic is the first stage of priestly formation. After having been accompanied by Vocation Ministry, candidates enter the seminary to experience community and pastoral life for a year, seeking greater vocational discernment. It is also a year of studies, with the aim of preparing seminarians for the Philosophy course.

Philosophy

The course meets the determinations of the National Curriculum Guidelines for the Philosophy Course, as well as the legislation that governs education in Brazil. It is a course that makes it possible to study the main problems, systems and authors of the philosophical tradition and encourages students to build knowledge and develop their intellectual autonomy, favoring the continuation of studies at the postgraduate level.

Theology

It is a Course that works from the perspective of the dialogue God - Human Being, both historically located. It is a systematic Course, while presenting a panoramic view of Theology and learning his method. It is the articulation of human life with historical reality from the faith. It seeks to define the mediations between faith and reality. Theological study, therefore, aims at a critical engagement through student reflection and action and the activation of their sensitivity, creativity and responsibility in relation to current problems.

What is a priest's day like?

Why?

The priestly and consecrated vocation therefore presents itself as a providential election of God, profoundly gratuitous, unforeseen and disproportionate to our calculations and human possibilities.
The priestly vocation is the greatest gift that God can deposit in souls. In the same way that he called Peter, Saint James, John… and said to them: ‘Come and follow me’, one day Christ fixed his gaze on a young man and said: ‘Come, I will make you fishers of men’. No one responded to the priesthood through human action, but because Christ himself, in the interior of their souls, pronounced his name and invited them to follow him. It is an invitation to great things: what is better than being an ambassador for God himself?
The priestly vocation is a call from God to some men within the people of God to become a representative of the sacred as the word itself indicates: sacerdos means sacred and otis means representative. Therefore, a priest is a representative of the sacred among the people.
